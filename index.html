Bacherlor in Computer Technology

The BACT is a four-year degree program thath focuses on the practical aspect of the sciences of computing. The program develops core skills in multimedia technology. web/mobile development and network/systems administration. Furthermore, it prepares learners with supervisory and management skills. The curriculum is derived fromt he BS in Industrial Technology major in Computer Technology with CHED Certificate of Program Compliance (COPC) No.31, series of 2021.

Specific Objectives

The Learner Should Be Able to:
    1. Demonsrate understanding of the importance of being an ethical computing professional when making work-related decisions.
    2. Develop products or processes by applying their knowledge in computer or network systems, and development of productivity tools.
    3. Participate as an active member of a development service team with leadership roles when appropriate.
    4. Apply communication skills effectively to promote ideas, goals processes or products.
    5. Make positive contributions to the community with the skills and abilities learned in the program.
    6. Pursue lifelong learning for personal and professional growth through varied experiential and field-based opportunities.
